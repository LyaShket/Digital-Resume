Launched alpha version of Riqli.com

  • Site link

I worked on a project called Riqli.com, a quality management system designed to help individuals and businesses improve themselves and their operations. The platform offers a range of tools, including job interview preparation, self-development resources, HR management, risk assessment, quality control, and audit tools.

During my time on the project, I helped launch the alpha version of the platform with my team. Our platform aims to provide a user-friendly and comprehensive experience for our users, empowering them to create their own strategies and achieve their realistic goals. By gathering and analyzing information from a variety of sources, such as articles, books, videos, and user feedback, we've created a knowledge base with logical chains and insights that users can leverage to develop their unique plans for success.
